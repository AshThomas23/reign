FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is my financial data safe?
In this demo, all your data stays in your browser — nothing is uploaded. In the full version, we use bank-level encryption, never sell your data, and never train AI on your individual transactions. You can export and delete everything anytime.
Do I need to connect my bank?
No. You can paste transactions or upload a CSV from your bank. We don't ask for your bank login. (Bank sync via Plaid is on the roadmap if you want it.)
How is this different from Mint or YNAB?
Other apps tell you what you spent. We tell you what to do about it. The "Can I afford this?" button intervenes before you spend. The AI coach gives blunt advice based on your actual data. The Goal Planner shows realistic timelines. Most apps are passive. We're not.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. One click in settings. No phone calls, no "are you sure?" guilt trips. We also have a 7-day money-back guarantee on Pro.
Is there a student discount?
Yes — 50% off Pro with a valid .edu email. We were students once too.
Who built this?
A solo dev who got tired of finance apps treating users like idiots and got equally tired of pretending to be "good with money." Made in Canada, 2026. Powered by AI and the suspicion that everyone's spending is worse than they think.

Privacy

Your data stays with you.

This demo build stores all your transaction data in your browser only. Nothing is sent to a server. Nothing is uploaded. If you clear your browser data, your transactions go with it.

The full version (with accounts and Pro features) will use industry-standard encryption and never share your data with third parties. We will never sell your information. We will never train AI on your individual data.
